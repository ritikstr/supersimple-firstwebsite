Why Dogs Make Great Pets

There are many reasons why dogs make great pets. They provide companionship, promote physical activity, and can even improve your mental health. Owning a dog can bring joy and happiness to your life.

Fun Facts About Dogs

  • Dogs have an exceptional sense of smell.
  • The Basenji dog breed doesn't bark.
  • Dogs can understand human emotions.
  • The average dog can run up to 19 miles per hour.
